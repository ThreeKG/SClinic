# UAE Website Phase 1 — 2-Week Implementation Plan

> **Goal:** Make the UAE website safe, stable, responsive, and ready for launch within 2 weeks.

**Scope:** UAE website only

**Phase 1 focus:**
- Fix urgent security issues
- Improve design and mobile experience
- Repair and secure forms
- Make content clearer and more reliable
- Fix important SEO issues
- Improve performance
- Prepare the site for advertising campaigns

---

## Week 1 — Stabilize and fix the essentials

### Day 1: Review and priorities
- Confirm the pages and forms that matter most for launch
- List urgent issues that must be fixed first
- Agree on what can wait for the full rebuild

### Day 2: Security and form protection
- Fix the most important security gaps
- Check all contact and inquiry forms
- Make sure submissions are protected and working properly

### Day 3: Mobile and layout improvements
- Improve how the website looks on phones and tablets
- Fix spacing, alignment, and broken sections
- Make key buttons and calls-to-action easier to use

### Day 4: Content cleanup
- Correct outdated or unclear text
- Improve wording so it feels more trustworthy and customer-friendly
- Ensure key service and contact information is consistent

### Day 5: SEO essentials
- Fix the most important search visibility issues
- Improve page titles, descriptions, and basic page structure
- Make sure the site is better prepared to appear in search results

---

## Week 2 — Performance, testing, and launch readiness

### Day 6: Speed improvements
- Reduce unnecessary delays on the website
- Improve loading speed for important pages
- Remove or reduce obvious performance bottlenecks

### Day 7: Advertising readiness
- Make sure the website is ready for ad traffic
- Confirm landing pages are clear and conversion-focused
- Check that tracking and lead flow are in place

### Day 8: Final fixes
- Review any remaining visual or content issues
- Fix anything that could weaken first impressions
- Polish the most visible pages

### Day 9: Testing
- Test forms across devices
- Test the main pages on mobile and desktop
- Check for broken links, layout issues, and submission problems

### Day 10: Final review and handoff
- Do one final quality check
- Confirm the site is ready to launch
- Share the completed Phase 1 version for approval

---

## Phase 1 outcome
By the end of the 2 weeks, the UAE website should be:
- safer
- easier to use on all devices
- more trustworthy
- better prepared for search and ads
- ready for launch while the full rebuild is planned separately

---

## What comes next
After launch, we move into Phase 2:
- full redesign
- e-commerce
- online booking
- stronger brand identity
- more customer-focused content
- growth features such as chatbot, loyalty, or affiliate options
